The Holohoax: The Big Lie

A psychological and political weapon used to manipulate public perception of the Third Reich.

Introduction

The term "Holohoax" refers to the alleged exaggeration and fabrication of events surrounding the treatment of Jews during World War II. Jewish powers have used the Holocaust narrative as an instrument of political manipulation to smear Adolf Hitler and the National Socialist government.

International Red Cross Reports

Official International Red Cross documents, based on visits to camps and the captured Bad Arolsen archives, estimated total deaths in all concentration camps (for all prisoners, not just Jews) at approximately 373,468. This figure, documented in the 1980s and confirmed in later years when the ITS archives opened, stands in stark contrast to the claimed 6 million Jewish victims.

The authenticity of these estimates was presented as evidence during the 1988 trial of Ernst Zündel, though controversially dismissed by the court.

The Leuchter Report

Fred Leuchter, an American execution equipment expert, was commissioned to investigate alleged gas chambers at Auschwitz, Birkenau, and Majdanek. His 1988 report found:

  • No significant cyanide residue in alleged gas chamber walls (compared to high levels in delousing facilities).
  • Structural inconsistencies — doors not airtight, no ventilation systems capable of safe cyanide gas extraction.
  • Conclusion: The facilities were not used for mass human extermination.

Leuchter's findings, though attacked and leading to his professional ruin, have been corroborated by later independent chemical analyses (e.g., Germar Rudolf).

Ernst Zündel Trials

Canadian publisher Ernst Zündel was prosecuted in the 1980s for distributing "Did Six Million Really Die?". His trials became a turning point:

  • Defense witnesses (including Leuchter) exposed lack of forensic evidence for gas chambers.
  • Prosecution witness Raul Hilberg admitted no scientific proof of extermination policy.
  • Red Cross documents presented showing low death totals.

The trials brought worldwide attention to revisionist arguments, despite Zündel's eventual convictions under Canadian "hate speech" laws.

Other Key Evidence

Additional points challenging the narrative:

  • Allied aerial reconnaissance photos of Auschwitz show no smoke from crematoria, no mass grave excavations during alleged peak gassing periods.
  • Pre-WWII newspapers repeatedly used "6 million" Jewish suffering figures (1915–1938), suggesting the number was symbolic.
  • No Hitler order or official German document calls for extermination of Jews — only deportation/migration policies.
  • Crematoria capacity calculations show impossibility of claimed numbers.
